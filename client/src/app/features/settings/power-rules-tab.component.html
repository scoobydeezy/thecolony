Influence

Controls how strongly faction peers can shift a character's conviction via the influence system.

Conviction Scale
Default: 0.5 — bonus = factionInfluenceAvg × (impressionable / 100) × scale

Faction Character Influence

A faction's character influence is a weighted blend of the average and maximum member influence scores. Weights should sum to 1.

charInfluence = avg × memberAvgWeight + max × memberMaxWeight
Default: 0.6
Default: 0.4

Total Faction Influence

A faction's total influence combines its base influence, character influence, and normalized momentum. Weights should sum to 1.

totalInfluence = base × baseWeight + charInfluence × charWeight + momentum × momentumWeight
Default: 0.45
Default: 0.35
Default: 0.20

Faction Power

Scales total influence into effective power based on a faction's legitimacy score (0–100).

effectivePower = totalInfluence × (legitimacyBase + legitimacy / legitimacyScale)
Default: 0.5 — power floor at legitimacy 0
Default: 100 — full legitimacy doubles the base

Leaderless Penalty

If a faction has no alive Faction Leader, its effective power is multiplied by this value. Set to 1 to disable the penalty.

effectivePower × leaderlessPowerMultiplier (when no alive leader)
Default: 0.75 — 25% power reduction
