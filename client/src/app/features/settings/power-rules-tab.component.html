Faction Character Influence

A faction's character influence is a weighted blend of the average and maximum member influence scores. Weights should sum to 1.

charInfluence = avg × memberAvgWeight + max × memberMaxWeight

Organization

organization = charStrength × charWeight + assetInfluence × assetInfluenceWeight + normalizedMomentum × momentumWeight
Default: 0.40

Legitimacy

Legitimacy measures public trust — a weighted blend of the faction's base legitimacy and its asset legitimacy score.

legitimacy = baseLegitimacy × baseLegitimacyWeight + assetLegitimacy × assetLegitimacyWeight

Faction Power

Power combines Organization and Legitimacy. The legitimacy multiplier scales from a floor at legitimacy 0 up through legitimacyScale.

power = organization × (legitimacyBase + legitimacy / legitimacyScale) + powerModifier
Default: 0.5 — power floor when legitimacy = 0
Default: 100 — full legitimacy doubles the base

Leaderless Penalty

If a faction has no alive Faction Leader, its character strength is multiplied by this value. Set to 1 to disable the penalty.

charStrength × leaderlessPowerMultiplier (when no alive leader)
Default: 0.75 — 25% reduction to character strength
